## How It Works

Tell Iris what to test in plain English. Watch as it navigates your app with the grace of a real user. It sees what they see. Clicks what they click. Finds what they might miss.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Test Creation">
    Create tests using natural language
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test Execution">
    Iris runs tests automatically
  </Step>
  <Step title="Issue Detection">
    When issues are found:
    - Creates tickets in Jira/Linear automatically
    - Sends instant notifications to Slack
    - Generates comprehensive test reports
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reporting">
    Detailed reports are delivered via email/Slack including:
    - Steps to reproduce
    - Video recording of the test run
    - Complete context for issue replication
  </Step>
</Steps>
